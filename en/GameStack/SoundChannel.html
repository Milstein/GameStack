SoundChannel Class

A single sound channel through which one sound at a time may be played.

Syntax

public class SoundChannel : IWaitFor, IDisposable

Remarks

You may instantiate any number of sound channels, limited only by platform and hardware constraints.

Volume Property

Gets or sets the volume level.

Syntax

Value

A value between 0 or 1, with 0 being silent and 1 being maximum volume.

Remarks

Setting this property affects only this sound channel. Each sound channel controls its volume independently.
