SoundEffect Class

An individual sound effect asset, commonly a short sound snippet that will be buffered in memory.

Syntax

public class SoundEffect : IDisposable

Remarks

For longer sounds such as music tracks, consider the GameStack.StreamingOpusFile class. To play sound effects, use the GameStack.SoundChannel class.
