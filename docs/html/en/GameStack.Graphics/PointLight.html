PointLight Class

Simulates a light that sends rays in all directions from a specific point.

Syntax

public class PointLight : Light

Remarks

This type of light is commonly used to simulate a light bulb or other local light sources.

attenuation
The attenuation, or rate of decrease of intensity as a function of distance.

Remarks

When setting attenuation, X is the quadratic term, Y is the linear term, and Z is the constant term. The attenuation formula is attenuation = 1.0 / (quadratic * dist * dist + linear * dist + constant)
